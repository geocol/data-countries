Data types
~~~~~~~~~~

See
<https://github.com/manakai/data-web-defs/blob/master/doc/data-types.txt>
and
<https://github.com/geocol/data-jp-transport/blob/master/doc/datatypes.txt>.

The data type "latlon" represents a JSON array with two "number"
items, where the first number is the latitude and the second number is
the longitude.  They identifies a WGS84 geographical position on the
Earth.
